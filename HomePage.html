What are moods?
Quote about moods or joke

A mood is a temporary state or feeling. Moods can last for hours or even days. A mood is a general feeling. You can be in a mood and not realize it. Moods and emotions are linked. When you are in a good mood you portray happiness, and excitement and other positive emotions. When you are in a bad mood you portray frustration, saddness, emptiness, anger, or similar negative feelings.
